- **Primary**: #2196F3 (Bright Blue) - Trust, technology, education

- **Accent 1**: #4CAF50 (Green) - Success, growth

### Typography
- **Headlines**: Montserrat Bold, 24-36pt
- **Subheadings**: Montserrat SemiBold, 16-20pt

### Visual Hierarchy
1. Large, attention-grabbing headlines
2. Supporting icons and graphics
3. Concise bullet points
4. White space for breathing room
5. Bold CTAs that stand out
